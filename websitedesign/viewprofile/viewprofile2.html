About me

Mr.Richard Gosh is a counselling psychologist with five years of experience in the field of mental health. She holds an MSc in counselling psychology and has worked with children, adolescents, young adults, and families facing various mental health challenges. Her expertise lies in dealing with academic-related issues, interpersonal relationships, relationship conflicts, stress management, and understanding the role of family dynamics in daily functioning. Mrs. Richa Gosh believes that the Counselor-Client relationship is built on trust, empathy, and respect, and she works towards fostering these qualities in her sessions.
