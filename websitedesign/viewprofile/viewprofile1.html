About me

Ms. Megha Sharma, a compassionate counselling psychologist, is dedicated to supporting individuals on their path to emotional well-being and personal growth. Her approach is characterised by unwavering compassion and a non-judgmental stance. She employs evidence-based therapeutic methods customised to cater to the specific needs of each individual. With empathy, passion, and dedication, she assists her clients in formulating successful strategies and solutions that foster healing, personal development, and constructive transformations. Her specialised area of proficiency encompasses providing guidance and counselling for a range of challenges, including anxiety, anger management, stress alleviation, depression coping, navigating breakups, resolving relationship conflicts, and addressing workplace stress.
