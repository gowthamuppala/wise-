About me

Meet Dr. Dhanunjay Sharma, a seasoned therapist dedicated to helping individuals overcome challenges and achieve personal growth. With a focus on OCD, addiction, and career-related issues, Dr. Sharma brings a wealth of experience and expertise to her practice. Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD) can significantly impact one's daily life, causing distress and interfering with relationships and work. Dr. Sharma employs evidence-based therapeutic techniques to help clients manage intrusive thoughts and compulsive behaviours effectively. He offers personalised treatment plans tailored to each client's unique needs and circumstances. Whether it's substance abuse, gambling addiction, or behavioural addictions, and provides a supportive environment where clients can explore underlying issues, develop coping strategies, and build resilience on their journey to sobriety and wellness. Mr. Sharma assists clients in clarifying their career goals, identifying strengths and interests, and addressing any barriers to success. Through career counselling, skills development, and stress management techniques, he empowers individuals to make informed decisions, pursue fulfilling careers, and thrive in their professional lives.
